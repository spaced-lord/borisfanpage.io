BORIS
Boris is a Japanese band formed in 1992 in the city of Tokyo. Using a very experimental approach they combine punk, stoner rock, J-pop, pschydelic rock, drone and others. The band often chooses to employ experimental instuments such as a theremin and double neck guitars. They have released 27 albums since their first in 1996, Absolutego.
